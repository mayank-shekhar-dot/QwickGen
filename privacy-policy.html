01 Overview & Introduction

Welcome to QuickGenAI ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy governs your use of the QuickGenAI website located at https://quickgenai.in and all associated tools, content, and services we provide (collectively, "the Platform").

QuickGenAI is an online platform offering free AI tools, AI image generation, educational AI content, productivity tools, and blogging resources. We are deeply committed to protecting the privacy of our users and operating transparently.

By accessing or using QuickGenAI, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Platform. This policy should be read alongside our Terms & Conditions, Disclaimer, and Cookie Policy.

🔑 Key Point: QuickGenAI does not require account registration or login for normal use. We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data from our visitors.

02 Information We Collect

While QuickGenAI is designed to be minimally invasive with respect to data collection, certain types of information may be collected automatically or when you voluntarily provide it.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit QuickGenAI, our servers and third-party analytics providers may automatically collect certain non-personally-identifying information, including:

  • Your browser type and version
  • Your device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Your operating system
  • Your approximate geographic region (country/region level, not precise location)
  • Pages you visit on QuickGenAI and time spent on those pages
  • The website that referred you to QuickGenAI
  • Date and time of your visits
  • Interaction data (clicks, scrolls) for analytics purposes

Information You Voluntarily Provide

If you use our contact form or send us an email, you may provide:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The content of your message

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

AI Tool Input Data

When you use our AI tools or image generation features, text prompts or inputs you provide may be processed by third-party AI service providers to generate outputs. We do not store or permanently retain this input data on our servers. Please do not submit sensitive personal information through AI tool inputs.

03 What We Do Not Collect

We want to be explicit about what we do NOT collect:

  • We do not collect your full name unless voluntarily provided via contact form
  • We do not collect payment or financial information (all tools are free)
  • We do not collect government-issued identification numbers
  • We do not collect passwords or account credentials (no login required for normal use)
  • We do not collect biometric data
  • We do not collect precise geolocation data
  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13
  • We do not collect health or medical information
  • We do not sell personal data to third parties under any circumstances

⚠️ Important: Please do not submit sensitive personal information (such as social security numbers, financial data, or medical records) through our AI tools, contact form, or any other input on this platform.

04 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

QuickGenAI uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. A cookie is a small text file stored on your device when you visit a website. We use cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic functioning of our website. They allow navigation, form submissions, and core features to work. Without these cookies, some parts of the website may not function properly.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our website. This data helps us improve our platform, identify popular content, and optimize performance. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymous.

Advertising Cookies

QuickGenAI uses Google AdSense and may use other advertising networks. These services place cookies on your device to deliver relevant advertisements based on your browsing history and interests. Advertising cookies help us sustain our free services.

Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on our platform.

For more detailed information about cookies, please read our full Cookie Policy.

05 Advertising & Analytics

Google AdSense

QuickGenAI participates in the Google AdSense program. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other sites on the internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. You may also opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies by visiting www.aboutads.info.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze the use of our website. Google Analytics gathers information about website use by means of cookies. The information gathered relating to our website is used to create reports about the use of our website. Google's privacy policy is available at Google Privacy Policy.

Other Analytics Providers

We may use other analytics tools from time to time. All analytics data collected is used only to improve our platform and understand user behavior in aggregate. We do not combine analytics data with personally identifiable information.

QuickGenAI integrates with and links to various third-party services. These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them:

  • Google AdSense — Advertising platform
  • Google Analytics — Web analytics service
  • AI Model Providers — Services that power our AI tools
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) — For delivering web assets
  • Third-party websites linked from our content

QuickGenAI is not responsible for the privacy practices of any third-party websites, services, or platforms linked from our content. We encourage users to read the privacy policies of any external services they use.

📌 When you click on links to third-party websites, you leave QuickGenAI and your interactions are governed by those websites' privacy policies, not ours.

07 AI-Generated Images & Content

When you use QuickGenAI's image generation tool, you provide text prompts that are processed to generate images. Please note:

  • Text prompts you enter may be transmitted to AI model providers for processing
  • Generated images are produced for your use and may be used for personal or commercial purposes
  • We do not permanently store your prompts or generated images on our servers unless technically necessary for delivery
  • Do not include personal, sensitive, or confidential information in your image prompts
  • AI-generated content may occasionally reflect biases present in training data

08 Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the data collected by our platform. These measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers
  • Secure server configurations and regular security reviews
  • Limiting access to collected data to only those who need it to operate the platform
  • Regular updates to software and security patches

However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Users transmit data at their own risk.

09 Children's Privacy

QuickGenAI is not directed toward children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at contactmayankproduction@gmail.com and we will promptly delete such information from our records.

If you are under 13 years of age, you should not use QuickGenAI without verified parental consent and supervision.

10 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding personal data. These may include:

  • Right to Access: Request information about the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of personalized advertising via Google's Ad Settings
  • Right to Restrict Cookies: Disable cookies through browser settings

Since QuickGenAI does not require account registration and collects minimal personal data, most users will have very little personal data on file with us. To exercise any rights, contact us at contactmayankproduction@gmail.com.
