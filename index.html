Mistakes Made by New Bloggers On SEO Within The First Month of Blogging

Starting a new blog can be exciting, however, many inexperienced bloggers make mistakes within the first month that will slow down the growth of their new blog that they do not know. These mistakes happen frequently and can be prevented.

Targeting Competitive Keywords

A popular mistake made by inexperienced bloggers is targeting competitive keywords. New bloggers often go after broad searches, i.e., "best AI tools" or "make money on the internet." Those types of searches are primarily owned and managed by giant websites. Rather than this, new bloggers should use long-tail keywords which are specific and easier to rank for.

Neglecting On-Page SEO

Another mistake made by inexperienced bloggers is neglecting proper on-page search engine optimization. Many new bloggers are not doing any type of optimization with their title tags, descriptions, heading tags (H1, H2), or alt text. Even great content could have difficulty ranking if the page is not clear in what it is about.

Poor Internal Linking

Furthermore, poor internal linking is a popular mistake made by many new bloggers. New bloggers will create and publish their post but fail to link their other posts together. Internal links will help Google crawl your website more easily and allow users to spend more time on your website.

Expecting Immediate SEO Results

New bloggers will often make the mistake of changing the content of their websites too frequently because they are looking for immediate success. However, SEO is a long process. Making changes frequently, i.e., changing URLs, deleting posts, concentrating on other areas will confuse search engines and slow you down.
