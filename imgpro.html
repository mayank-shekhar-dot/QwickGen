FAQ

Common Questions

PixelForge uses Google's Gemini image generation model, one of the most advanced multimodal AI systems available today. It excels at understanding nuanced prompts and generating high-quality imagery across a wide range of styles.
No Google account is required to use PixelForge. The Gemini API key is configured by the site operator on the backend. Simply visit the site, enter your prompt, and generate images immediately.
Most images generate within 5–15 seconds depending on the complexity of your prompt and current server load. Simpler prompts tend to generate faster, while detailed scenes with specific styles may take a bit longer.
Yes — every successfully generated image is automatically saved to your gallery. You can view, download, or delete images from the gallery section at any time. The gallery persists as long as the server is running.
Usage rights depend on Google's Gemini API terms of service. Generally, images generated via the API are yours to use, but review the latest Gemini API usage policies for commercial use guidelines and any restrictions that may apply.
Images are returned in PNG format by default. You can request different aspect ratios (1:1, 16:9, 9:16, 4:3) via the ratio selector. The AI will compose the scene to fit the selected proportions as closely as possible.
