AI for Dreams: Could Future Smartphones Interpret Your Sleeping Patterns into Stories?

Your phone uses algorithms to interpret all the data it collects in addition to using machine learning techniques to create more intelligent results as time progresses. All the information gathered is fed to your phone using the most sophisticated data-processing methods available today. While AI will not replace the dreaming function of your subconscious, it may give your phone a new level of creativity to help it create a more meaningful dream for you.

Using Technology to Dream — Potential Applications of Sleep Data

As AI develops, it will likely become possible to create many different types of dreams for each person based on how they sleep each night. Depending on the person and how they correlate with their sleeping habits, the possibilities of one's dream may become very different than how they actually dream.

In addition to identifying common themes within individuals' dreams, we may be able to derive new insights into the relationships between dream content and reality. As technology continues to develop, more and more people may find it easier to take advantage of their sleeping habits. A few of the common applications for AI and sleep pattern analysis will include:

  • Creating custom dream stories based on user input
  • Understanding dream emotions
  • Creating custom-created dream analytics based on previous experiences
  • Designing dream team building activities for the workplace
  • Identifying potential triggers for waking dreams

Future AI Pipeline: Transfer Your Sleep to A Story

Picture yourself waking up, looking at your phone, and seeing:

"Your Night Story is Ready."

You click on it, and the AI creates a poetic or magical realism fairy tale based on your sleep patterns.

Here’s how the proposed system would work:

1. Collect Sleep Data (Already Happens)

Your mobile devices and/or smartwatches monitor your:

  • HR trend
  • sleep cycles (light/deep)
  • movement spikes
  • ambient noises

(no scanning of your brain - just looking at what you physically did).

2. Emotional Patterns Predictions Based On Collected Data

The phone will predict, based upon the rise and fall of one's activity, a general feeling about that person:

  • Alright
  • Ok
  • Ecstatic
  • Energized
  • Excited
  • Nothingness
  • Chaos
  • Surprises
  • Adventures
  • Serenity
  • Spiritual

Fitness watches already track the level of stress an individual experiences through their heart patterns.

3. Story Generator For Your Dreams

Artificial Intelligence takes over as Storyteller.

By utilizing your sleep patterns and emotional tones in conjunction with the micro-model created by the Artificial Intelligence, the phone could devise the following types of scenarios for you, based upon your data from your sleep activities:

  • A peaceful Fantasy Scene
  • An Imagination Adventure
  • A Reality Distortion
  • A Representation in Nature
  • A Relaxing Diary Entry From A Dreamer

These will not be your dreams but will be Stories Based Upon The Artificial Intelligence's Interpretation of Your Sleep Data.

4. Elements Of The Story Based Upon Your Preferences

As Artificial Intelligence progresses, it could be tailored to reflect your specific likes, such as:

  • Your Favorite Colors
  • Locations You Like
  • Calm Sceneries
  • Characters Based Upon Your Likes

But will contain no references to information obtained from Private Messages or Personal Material, Only Information Obtained From The Happy Quiet Times.

This is similar to the Theme Choices For Your Themes And Widgets on a smartphone.

5. A Morning Review Of Your Dreams

Upon awakening, the phone would present the user with:

  • A One Minute Dream Story
  • A Short Poem Based On Your Sleep Arcs
  • A Surreal Animation Based Upon Your Sleep Data
  • A Calming Narrative That Will Help You Start Your Day

These will be Artistic Soft Experiences Of Dream Scenarios Based Upon Your Sleep Data, Not An Attempt To Read Your Dreams Literally.

Safety & Ethics (No Mind Reading)

First, it is essential that future dream-based AI focuses strictly on the biological data, not psychological data, representing the individual.

So that means

  • ❌ No analysing your private thoughts
  • ❌ No trying to guess a person's memories
  • ❌ No interpreting someone's fears or secrets
  • ❌ No making a diagnosis based on medical or mental health

And Instead

  • ✔ Telling stories based only on sleep pattern
  • ✔ Measuring your physical signals with your phone safely
  • ✔ Being the choice of the user, i.e. They will be able to customise it any way they wish.

Dreams are reminders that we are all creative beings, even when we are sleeping. Dream storytelling through AI will not replace actual dreaming; it will celebrate the artistic creations of those who have dreamed. Your nightly sleep will offer the potential for inspiration.
