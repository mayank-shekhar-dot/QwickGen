How Indian Students Can Use AI Safely for Study

Utilizing Artificial Intelligence as a Tool to Understand Rather Than Copying

Artificial Intelligence is beneficial for:

  • Simplifying complex ideas into easy language
  • Illustrating complicated concepts with illustrations
  • Separating complicated chapters into a list of concepts for studying
  • Summarizing NCERT material without substituting it.

Avoid copying responses to homework or tests directly from AI, as teachers will know.

Instead, ask an AI to tutor you on the material, then reword the responses for your homework or exam.

Utilizing Artificial Intelligence to Create Your Study Plan Rather than Study for You

You can create a:

  • Daily study calendar
  • Review schedule
  • Plan by chapter, based upon a topic
  • Prepare for tests by chapter
  • Identifying your weak topic(s)

Using Artificial Intelligence as your study guide helps keep you organized without eliminating the need for you to work hard.

Utilizing Artificial Intelligence to Create Sample Practice Questions Rather than Using it for Cheating

You can generate:

  • Multiples choice questions
  • Practice exams
  • Sample exam questions
  • Sample Quiz Questions
  • Test flashcards

Using these resources will improve your retention and confidence.

Do not use AI for test-taking or assignments that require your own work.

Use Artificial Intelligence to Confirm Important Information

At times, the information provided by Artificial Intelligence can be out-of-date or incomplete. For instance:

  • Board examination questions
  • Entrance examination preparation
  • Dates/formula's/graphics
  • NCERT Answers

Make a habit of confirming all important information provided by Artificial Intelligence using reputable sources:

  • ✔ NCERT/Official Textbooks
  • ✔ Classroom teacher notes
  • ✔ Established Educational Websites

Use Artificial Intelligence to Build Multilingual Skills if Necessary

Artificial Intelligence works effectively in English, Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Kannada, Gujarati and Punjabi and Malayalam.

If you find the AI's explanation challenging because of the language barrier, request that AI, "Please use Hinglish to explain this to me."

The best way to gain knowledge is never to share private details.

⭐ 7. AI as a Tool to Enhance Creativity, Not for Its Replacement

AI assists with:

  • essay themes
  • outlines
  • feedback on work written by the user
  • examples

However, written final drafts should be solely authored by the writer, thereby preventing excessive reliance on artificial intelligence while further developing writing skills.

⭐ 8. AI Provides College and Career Support (Authenticated)

The following are areas where artificial intelligence can provide assistance:

  • career description
  • listing of leading universities
  • understanding of topics
  • determining career options available after the completion of 10th/12th grades

All AI-provided knowledge should be validated using:

  • ✔ official university or college website
  • ✔ governmental agencies (NIRF, JoSAA, etc.)
  • ✔ guidance provided by school counselors/teachers

⭐ 9. Reduce Stress with AI Instead of Increasing It

Artificial intelligence has the capacity to help those suffering from stress by:

  • dividing larger tasks into smaller steps
  • assisting in area of study/revision
  • providing incentives
  • providing explanations of areas with which one is not familiar with because of missing class

Do not compare yourself with others, nor should you feel pressure to perform.

⭐ 10. Become Aware of The Fundamental Concepts of How AI Operates

Understanding how ETI is utilized enables you to recognize false data.

Realize:

  • every ETI/prototypical application can contain errors
  • every ETI/prototypical application is unable to adequately assess an individual's score
  • ETIs/prototypical applications will not replace quality educators
  • you will develop necessary logical reasoning abilities

Being aware of how ETIs function provides safety.

🎯 Drain Ending

In conclusion, while AI cannot be viewed as a shortcut to success, it is viewed as a valuable resource.

AI will continue to remain helpful in the following types of endeavors:

  • a learning assistant
  • a motivational assistant
  • an organizational assistant
  • an illustrative explanatory assistant
  • a practice partner

On the other hand, AI is NOT intended for:

  • an assignment completion service
  • a cheating device
  • total substitution
