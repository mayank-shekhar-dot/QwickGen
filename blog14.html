AI Time Capsules — Storing Your Digital Personality for the Future

IA Time Capsule: How Technology Will Change Our Future Memory Storage

An example of a new form of technology that will help people keep track of their life experiences and their memories is simply data captured throughout a person's life and represented digitally, much like photo albums and journals. The IA Time Capsule (Intelligence assisted) concept allows users to build a digital representation of themselves and their lives, similar to how we preserve memories in the physical world. The difference is that due to the advanced level of AI, it allows for a higher level of intelligence than traditional ways to preserve memories.

What is the IA Time Capsule?

IA Time capsule will allow users to create a digital storage container for their various forms of communication, including, but not limited to, emails, SMS (text messaging), social media posts (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram), as well as photos, videos, and other forms of media. Once the container has been filled with the user's data, the IA Time Capsule can analyze the information, identify patterns in the user's life and then utilize those patterns to build a complete digital memory of the user's life and experiences.

An example of how the IA Time Capsule could be utilized: If you visit a destination and take photographs of the sights during that trip, you could then store the photographs and videos from your trip in your IA Time Capsule. The IA may display the highlights of that trip, the music associated with that trip, and how to interact with your travel companions based on the data stored in your IA Time Capsule.

How Does it Work?

Collection of Digital Data

The IA Time Capsule collects your personal digital data through collecting your digital communication (chats, emails, photos, and videos), as well as your daily routines.

Analyzing and Understanding Digital Data

The IA Time Capsule will analyze the data collected from your life and identify which memories are the most significant to you.

Synthesizing Digital Memories

The IA Time Capsule can summarize the digital memories created by analyzing the collected digital data and create a digital memory for you using your IA Time Capsule.
